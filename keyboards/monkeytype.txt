4. COMPARATIVE INSIGHTS & PERFORMANCE DRIVERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Aula F87 demonstrated a clear performance advantage, outperforming the Razer 
Huntsman v3 Pro Mini by an average of 6.50 WPM (an 11.2% speed increase) while 
maintaining superior accuracy and consistency. This delta can be directly 
attributed to two primary hardware factors:

* The Tactile Advantage (Akko V3 Cream Blue Switches):
  Unlike the linear feel of the Razer Optical switches, the Akko Cream Blues 
  provide a distinct tactile bump right at the actuation point. This physical 
  feedback acts as a confirmation to the fingers that a keystroke has registered, 
  allowing for a faster lift-off and transition to the next key. On the Razer, 
  the 1.6 mm actuation point without physical feedback likely led to slight 
  "bottoming out" or hesitation, slowing down total typing cadence.

* Ergonomics and Grip (ASA Profile Keycaps):
  The ASA profile on the Aula F87 features a larger, more spherical, cupped 
  surface on top of each keycap compared to the flatter, lower-profile Cherry 
  shape on the Razer. This cupped design naturally centers the fingertips during 
  rapid bursts of typing, preventing minor slips and increasing overall confidence. 
  This superior finger-housing directly correlates with the higher 68.25% 
  consistency score observed during the tests.

* Precision vs. Sensitivity:
  The slight dip in the Razer's execution metrics suggests that its hyper-sensitive 
  optical analog sensors are prone to micro-movement registers (accidental key 
  presses), whereas the deliberate force required to pass the tactile bump of the 
  Aula F87 acted as a natural barrier against typos.


5. CONCLUSION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Based on the filtered empirical data, the Aula F87 configuration serves as a 
substantially more efficient and stable platform for dedicated text-entry. The 
pairing of tactile switches with deep, scooped ASA profile keycaps creates an 
optimized feedback loop that fundamentally favors rhythmic speed and muscle memory 
accuracy over linear optical alternatives.
